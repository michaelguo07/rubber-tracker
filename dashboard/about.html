About the Application

Equipment Tracking

Table tennis rubbers are expensive. And the fact that most beginners need to consult a coach to see whether they need to switch rubbers is a hassle. So TTInsights montiors their training time and logs the time played with thier rubber. Then it will see the next time the athlete switches rubber, creating a personalized timelime of when you should switch rubbers.

Health Analysis

Integrates with the Google Health API v4 (Fitbit / Pixel Watch / Apple Watch) to extract training sessions, heart rate intensity zones, active calories expended, and steps taken during table tennis play. This helps players analyze physical load and optimize recovery.

Trend Finder

Provides automated scanning for session inconsistencies (such as abnormally short/long training logs) and gaps in practice frequency. This keeps training schedules transparent and consistent.

Coach and Parent Integration

Includes a student/parent/coach portal so everyone can see the same data and insights. This ensures everyone can monitor the athlete's training and equipment health, leading to better communication and decision-making.
