About the Application

🏓 Equipment Tracking

Table tennis rubber sheets degrade over time as they are exposed to friction, dust, and light. TTInsights monitors cumulative training time logged on each active rubber sheet, allowing athletes to replace their equipment before performance drops.

⚡ Health & Fitness Analytics

Integrates with the Google Health API v4 (Fitbit / Pixel Watch) to extract training sessions, heart rate intensity zones, active calories expended, and steps taken during table tennis play. This helps players analyze physical load and optimize recovery.

🔍 Anomaly & Trend Detection

Provides automated scanning for session inconsistencies (such as abnormally short/long training logs) and gaps in practice frequency. This keeps training schedules transparent and consistent.
